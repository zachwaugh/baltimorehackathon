The First
Baltimore Hackathon

  • Build.

  • Meet People.

  • Have Fun.

What is this?

Build a hardware or software project from idea to prototype in a weekend!

You can participate individually or as a team.

When?

November 19-21 2010. The event will start at 6pm on Friday 11/19 and end at 6pm on Sunday 11/21

Where?

It will be held at Beehive Baltimore, 2400 Boston St, on the 3rd floor of the Emerging Technology Center.


Rules

The rules are evolving on the wiki. We encourage you to add to the wiki and also ask questions there. The rules will be considered final when opening ceremonies begin.

Awards

Judges will be recognizing the best projects in these categories:

  • Best Software Prototype - Individual $150
  • Best Software Prototype - Group $350
  • Best Hardware Prototype - Individual $150
  • Best Hardware Prototype - Group $350
  • Audience Favorite $150

Door prizes which will be awarded via random drawing:

  • iPod nano from Mac Medics
  • Seat at Funding Universe funding university
  • Seat at Netrino's Embedded Software Software Camp

How do I participate?

You can register for the event on its EventBrite page
